Introduction

Samuel is a student of the National Taiwan University Computer Science and Information Technology's Training Course. He is currently learning about both Java and HTML, both of which are essential components towards understanding more about how computers work. The current project you can currently see is one of his completed works from the HTML class.

In addition to being a post-graduate student from Rosalind Franklin University of Medicine and Science, he resides in Los Angeles, California, USA.

Favorite Foods

Samuel likes to eat a diet consisting of healthy foods that are both nutritious and filling. These foods give him energy to perform his daily tasks, such as exercising or going to class. He therefore likes to eat many types of meat as a protein source, including chicken, beef, lamb, and eggs. He also likes to eat fruits and vegetables, some notable fruits being mangoes and apples.

Given that his parents were from Taiwan, Samuel likes to eat a lot of Taiwanese foods as well. This includes stinky tofu, Taiwanese sausage, and beef noodle soup, just to name a few.

There were some foods that became his favorite recently, such as fried milk balls, Taiwanese tempura, and Vietnamese Banh Mi sandwich.

Favorite Hobbies

Samuel likes to keep an active lifestyle to promote his lifelong mission of promoting health and wellness both through himself and through his future career goals as a physician. Therefore, he has many hobbies involving physical activity, such as swimming, basketball, snowboarding, and weightlifting. Furthermore, he has additional hobbies to complement his active lifestyle, including playing the piano and working on automobiles. He has shown interest in most of these hobbies since he was a child, and his interest has only became greater as he matured.

Having a set of hobbies such as those above have allowed Samuel to relax from his daily routine, while allowing him to express himself outside of his daily schedule. He continues to enjoy these hobbies today.

Other Information

Samuel is the oldest of three children in his immediate family, with one younger sister and one younger brother as his siblings. Evelyn is his younger sister, and Milton is his younger brother. They are also taking the NTU Computer Training Course along with him as well.

Although he has grown up in Los Angeles, CA for most of his life, he has been to school at Rosalind Franklin University of Medicine and Science in North Chicago, IL for one year to work on his Master's in Biomedical Sciences.
